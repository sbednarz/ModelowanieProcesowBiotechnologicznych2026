FAKE UV-Vis kinetics dataset: A -> B

Contents:
- spectrum_t00.csv ... spectrum_t09.csv : 10 individual spectra
- all_spectra_wide.csv : one table, wavelength in first column, spectra in next columns
- all_spectra_long.csv : long/tidy format
- preview_plot.png : quick preview

Parameters:
- Spectral range: 200-500 nm
- Step: 1 nm
- Number of spectra: 10
- Artificial data only
- Designed to mimic a reaction A -> B with noise and slight baseline drift
